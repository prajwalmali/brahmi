Welcome to BHRAMI

With the improvement of technology and the miniaturization of sensors, attempts are being made to apply new technologies in various fields to improve the quality of people's lives. A major area of research where this technology has been adopted is in the healthcare field. People who need medical care find the cost of medical care very high, especially in developing countries. It is classified in three categories. The first part is to use the sensors to detect the patient's vital signs. The second part is to send the data to cloud storage, and the last part is to be able to view the detected data remotely and provide emergency services. Remote viewing of data allows physicians or parents to monitor patient health from a distance from hospital facilities. Health monitoring for active and cared living is one of the paradigms in which IoT can be used to improve patient lifestyles. The proposed architecture therefore collects sensor data via a microcontroller and transfers it to the cloud. In the cloud it is processed and analysed for remote viewing.
